## Cadence rules

- Every line lands on a frame boundary (30 fps → multiples of 1/30s).
- No cut lingers >1.2s. Average shot length ≈ 0.6s.
- Captions: max 3 words per card, bottom-third, white with black stroke, all-caps Inter Black.
- Music: optional bed — currently the SFX stack carries the energy.
